TERM 2 PROJECTS






Year-Long




CareForWelfare

- Aids students to share concerns regarding welfare directing them to appropriate personnel for proper resolution while ensuring confidentiality.

Platforms: Google Sheets, Google Forms, catch2t27.com website, Zoom, Facebook and Instagram Pages







Catch2t27.com

- A platform where CCS ID 123 students may access all of the information that they need in regards to their concerns. The goal is to create a safe environment where students may revisit previous events, find the right links to the right resources (primers, usg announcements, etc...). And to create a venue to implement projects such as #Include Society. The website will use Google Firebase as its web hosting provider which is free for small services. No database would be needed, the only cost would be the domain name catch2t27.com.







#include <society>

- ”#Include Society” is a campaign mainly focused on informing CCS students about national affairs through posts wherein the latest and biggest news are informed within the batch. Majority of the news will often be CCS focused as it will still be relevant and related to the course.

Platforms: catch2t27.com website, Facebook and Instagram Pages
