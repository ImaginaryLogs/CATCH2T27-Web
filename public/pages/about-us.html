Who we are?
Computer Batch 2027 (CATCH2T27) is the representative body of ID123 undergraduate students of the College of Computer Studies.
Mission:
Our mission is to ignite curiosity, inspire innovation, and empower CCS 123 students to excel in coding and computer education, fostering a passion for lifelong learning in the dynamic realm of technology.
Vision:
Our envisions a batch that constantly innovates through their curiosity and passion for computer education.
